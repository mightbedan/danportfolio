GEIP OVERSEAS Internship

My 5 month's time as an intern in Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam

March 10, 2023 Dan Ng

During my last year as a Poly Student, I had the great opportunity to be selected to work overseas at a startup for my final year. At my company AirCity, located in the heart of Ho Chi Minh City, Vietnam, I was given the offer to work with their team as a Front End Engineer. During my time there, I have worked on projects ranging from websites to mobile applications that enhances the work process of fellow colleagues and consolidation and representing data in an effective manner.

In addition to working in a foreign and fast paced environment, I had the honors of chatting and showcasing our work to many high ranking delegates from Singapore such as DPM Heng & President Halimah during my internship period.
