Space Race Timeline

Our auto scroller interactive website (P.S. website was not optimised for mobile use)

February 21, 2021 Dan Ng

As part of one of our school projects, we created this website that summarises the timeline of the space race between the United States and the Soviet Union. Essentially we thought of the idea of creating a website that auto scrolls itself through the timeline which presents the information in a unique and easily digestable way unlike many encyclopedic websites. In addition we also decide to hide fun facts through the website which helps to engage our audience
